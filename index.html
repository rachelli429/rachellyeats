— about

Hi! 👋 I’m a freshman at UC Berkeley, intending to major in Statistics or Data Science. I’m Chinese-American and from Maryland.

Some things I love are journalism, making vlogs, puzzles, Disney movies, and food! I run a food Instagram and love finding new restaurants to try. I wanted a place where I could consolidate all the restaurants I’ve been to in an aesthetic fashion, which is why I’m making this website.

Some of my favorite foods include: boba (especially honeydew milk tea or peach green tea), sushi, poke, cheesecake, Sour Patch Kids, McDonald’s chicken nuggets, mac & cheese, and anything sweet!
